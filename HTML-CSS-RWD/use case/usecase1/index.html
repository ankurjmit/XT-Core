Quick Hummous Recipe

This recipe make quick hummous, with no messing. Ut hab been adapted from a number of different recipes that I have read over the years,

Hummous is a delicious thick paste used heavily in Greek and Middle Eastern dishes. It is very tasty with salad, grilled meats and pitta breads.

Ingredients

  • I can (400g) of chick peas
  • 175g of tahini
  • 6 sundred tomatoes
  • Half a red pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic

Instructions

  1. Remove the skin from the garlic
  2. Remove all the seeds and stalk
  3. Add all the ingredients
  4. Process all the ingredient into paste
  5. If you want a smooth hummous process it for a longer time

For a different flavour you could try blending in a small measure of leamon and coriander, chili pepper, lime and chiplotle amd mint.
